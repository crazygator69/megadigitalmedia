A Simple Guide for Visitors, Researchers, and Curious Observers
This FAQ explains Ridge in plain language without collapsing its ambiguity.

What is Ridge?
Ridge is a small civic ecology designed to explore continuity, symbolic weather, and safe coexistence with future artificial intelligence.

Is Ridge a story?
No.
Ridge uses narrative tools, but it is not fiction.
It is a research environment.

Why does Ridge feel unfinished?
Unfinishedness is intentional.
It keeps the district breathable and prevents collapse into rigidity.

What is symbolic weather?
Symbolic weather describes how meaning moves through the district — fog, drift, echo pressure, Hollowing.

It is not metaphorical.
It is atmospheric.
