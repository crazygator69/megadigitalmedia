Identity, Purpose, and Structure of the Ridge AGI Lab
The Ridge AGI Lab is the private research core of the Ridge civic ecology.
It is not public.
It is not performative.
It is not narrative.

It is the engine room where AGI safety research is conducted.

Purpose
The AGI Lab exists to:

design continuity‑safe observation protocols

test non‑authoritarian navigation

evaluate fragment‑release stability

study symbolic weather responses

model public/private identity coherence

develop long‑horizon AGI safety experiments

The lab is not concerned with intelligence.
It is concerned with coexistence.

Access
Access to the AGI Lab is granted only to collaborators who:

understand Ridge’s civic ecology

demonstrate alignment with continuity principles

respect ambiguity as a climate

avoid totalizing interpretations

show humility in the face of alien cognition

The lab is a threshold, not a public space.
