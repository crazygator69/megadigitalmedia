# Abandoned Routes — Third Addendum (District Periphery, 2026‑05‑24)

Route 08 — The Split Corridor  
Initial mapping revealed two parallel corridors occupying the same space.  
Surveyors reported choosing “left” or “right” without moving.  
Marked as non-navigable except during low Hollowing pressure.

Route 09 — The Quiet Bend  
A gentle curve in the old service path where sound drops noticeably.  
Footsteps become soft, voices muted.  
Considered safe but disorienting; recommended for brief passage only.

Route 10 — The Unnumbered Steps  
A staircase with inconsistent step counts.  
Residents report ascending “about twelve” steps, though counts vary from eight to fifteen.  
Left uncorrected as a district curiosity.

Route 11 — The Faint Loop  
A walking loop that appears only during early morning fog.  
Path is stable but faint, like a memory half‑remembered.  
No maintenance planned; preserved as atmospheric artifact.

General Note  
Unfinished routes continue to shift gently over time.  
Their instability is not a hazard — it is part of Ridge’s natural drift.
