# Abandoned Routes — District Periphery

Route 01 — The Old Service Tunnel  
Initial mapping began in 2019.  
Abandoned after repeated echo inconsistencies and a brief loss of orientation among surveyors.  
Tunnel remains accessible but unadvised.  
Markings fade and reappear without pattern.

Route 02 — The North Ridge Footpath  
Closed during the Drift years.  
Vegetation overgrowth accelerated beyond expected rates.  
Residents report the path “feels longer” than before.  
No official reopening planned.

Route 03 — The Lower Transit Connector  
Construction halted after Hollowing pressure caused signage to detach repeatedly.  
Connector now used informally as a shortcut, though distances vary by time of day.  
Maintenance crews check it monthly but avoid extended stays.

General Note  
Abandoned routes are preserved for reference, not restoration.  
Their instability is considered part of the district’s character.
