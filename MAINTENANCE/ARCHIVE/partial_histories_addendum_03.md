# Partial Histories — Third Addendum (Compiled 2026‑05‑24)

Entry 13  
A resident recalls a brief period when the Ridge “felt taller.”  
No architectural changes occurred.  
Likely a collective perception shift during a mild Hollowing cycle.

Entry 14  
An old note references “the evening the porches whispered.”  
Descriptions vary: some heard creaking, others soft murmurs.  
No consensus reached; preserved as atmospheric folklore.

Entry 15  
A maintenance ledger fragment lists a corridor that cannot be located today.  
Name: “The Narrow Return.”  
Its purpose is unknown, and no map includes it.

Entry 16  
Several elders remember a winter gathering involving lanterns shaped like small houses.  
No physical lanterns survive.  
Some believe the memory itself is the artifact.

General Note  
The Ridge’s history is not a timeline — it is a sedimentary field.  
Layers shift, overlap, and contradict without losing coherence.
