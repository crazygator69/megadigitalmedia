# Partial Histories — Compiled 2026-05-20

Entry 01  
Residents recall that the Ridge once had a central clock tower.  
No drawings survive.  
Some say it chimed during Hollowing storms; others insist it never worked.  
Foundation stones remain under the old transit stop.

Entry 02  
Before the Drift years, District 1 reportedly held weekly markets.  
Records conflict on what was traded.  
Most agree the stalls were arranged in a spiral, though the direction is disputed.

Entry 03  
A caretaker’s note from an archived ledger mentions “the year the stairs forgot their count.”  
No further explanation.  
Stairwell numbering has been inconsistent ever since.

Entry 04  
Several residents remember a winter with no snow but heavy symbolic pressure.  
Porch gatherings increased.  
Kettle logs from that season show unusually high tea consumption.

General Note  
These histories are maintained for continuity, not accuracy.  
Contradictions are preserved intentionally.
